Research Experience

Natural gas consumption forecasting using deep learning and MSTL
* Uses Multi-seasonal trend decomposition to decompose the time series of gas consumption volume into multiple seasonal components, a trend component, and a residual component.
* The trend and residual component are proceeded by global and local convolution, further followed by self-attention with mlp.
* Result is concluded by depersonalization of the seasonal components and the forecast results of trend and residual components.
* Methods applied to a city-level natural gas consumption dataset, overwhelm other forecasting methods like LSTNet.

Fault diagnosis using GAN and signal processing
* Proposed a two-step bearing fault diagnosis method.
* The first stage uses GAN to check out whether the bearing is at fault status.
* The second step is to proceed which further uses the signal analysis method to check out the characteristic frequency to locate where the fault is if a fault is detected.
* Method applied on CWRU bearing dataset and works out. Further experiment is needed for analysis.
